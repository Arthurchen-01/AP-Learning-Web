2. A survey was conducted to determine the state of the physical and psychological health of high school students.
Part A
Some of the survey questions related to student stress levels and student absences due to illness. The data on 
these variables are displayed below.  The figure presents a scatterplot consisting of 16 points. The horizontal axis is labeled “Stress Level.” The vertical axis is labeled “Number of Absences Due to Illness.” The points begin near the origin, and trend upward and to the right. There is a line of best fit drawn that begins at the origin and moves upward and to the right. There are 10 points above the line and 6 points below the line. The points are clustered fairly close around the line.
