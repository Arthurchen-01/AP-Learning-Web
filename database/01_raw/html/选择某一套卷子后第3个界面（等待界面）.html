AP Practice Test

Timing

This full-length practice test is timed like the real AP, but you can pause any time by selecting Save and Exit from the More menu. You can also move from one section to the next before time expires.

Scores

when you finish the practice test, go to My AP to see your scores and get personalized study tips.

Assistive Technology

If you use assistive technology, you should try it out on the practice test so you know what to expect on test day.
